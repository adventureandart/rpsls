Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock was built using AngularJS, Bootstrap, and Google Fonts (“Changa One” for the headings and “Roboto” for the body).

The icons in this app were acquired from the Noun Project:

  • Fist icon designed by Till Teenck
  • Rock icon designed by John Maravelakis
  • Paper icon designed by Dmitry Baranovskiy
  • Scissors icon designed by Lorena Salagre
  • Lizard icon designed by Ben Markoch
  • Vulcan salute icon designed by Till Teenck
